Frequently Asked Questions

Certainly! Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) tailored for an organization that owns and operates a blockchain-integrated Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, along with their answers

1. What exactly does your blockchain-integrated EHR system do?

Our blockchain-integrated EHR system securely stores and manages patient health records using blockchain technology. This ensures data integrity, security, and interoperability between different healthcare providers, enhancing patient care and administrative efficiency.

2. How does your system ensure the security and privacy of patient data?

Our system uses advanced encryption techniques and decentralized storage to ensure that patient data is secure and tamper-proof. Only authorized personnel can access specific records, and all access attempts are logged and auditable. Patients also have control over who can view their records.

3. What are the benefits of using your EHR system for healthcare providers?

Benefits for healthcare providers include improved data accuracy and security, streamlined sharing of patient information, reduced administrative costs, enhanced compliance with regulatory requirements, and better overall patient care coordination.

4. How can patients access their health records in your system?

Patients can access their health records through a secure web portal or mobile app. They can also manage permissions for who can access their data, giving them full control over their personal health information.

5. How can your system help reduce healthcare fraud?

Our blockchain-integrated EHR system helps reduce healthcare fraud by providing a transparent and immutable record of all transactions. This makes it difficult for unauthorized alterations or fraudulent activities to go undetected, thereby enhancing the overall integrity of patient data.
